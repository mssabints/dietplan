Weekly Low-Carb Meal Plan

Diabetic Tips & Substitutions

  • Cauliflower Rice: Swap white rice with cauliflower rice (pre-riced at Aldi/Lidl or grate fresh). Low-carb, low-GI.
  • Greek Yogurt: Use full-fat Greek yogurt instead of curd for more protein, fewer carbs. Available at Aldi, Lidl, SuperValu.
  • Almond Flour: Replace maida with almond flour for baking/coating (health food section at Aldi/Lidl).
  • Spices: Stock turmeric, cumin, coriander, chili powder at Aldi/Lidl for South Indian flavors.
  • Low-GI Veggies: Choose broccoli, spinach, zucchini, green beans, eggplant (widely available).
  • Healthy Fats: Cook with olive oil, coconut oil, or ghee (moderately), available at all stores.
  • Protein Boost: Add eggs, chicken, fish, paneer, tofu for satiety, found at Aldi, Lidl, SuperValu.
  • Portion Control: Small carb portions, paired with fiber-rich veggies for blood sugar control.
  • Check Labels: Avoid processed foods with hidden sugars (sauces, ready meals).

Notes

Ingredients available at Aldi, Lidl, SuperValu. Recipes use simple techniques (stir-fry, grill, one-pot). Pre-chop veggies or marinate proteins to save time. Adjust chili to taste. Keep carbs low (e.g., 1/4 cup lentils) and focus on non-starchy veggies, proteins for diabetes management.
